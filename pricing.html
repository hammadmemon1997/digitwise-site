Common Questions

Frequently asked.

Do you lock me into a contract?

+

No lock-in contracts. We work month-to-month, and you can cancel with 30 days notice. We earn your business every month.

What if my transaction volume changes?

+

We'll review your volume each month and adjust your plan accordingly — up or down. You'll always be on the right plan for your business size.

Do I need to provide my own accounting software?

+

We can work with your existing Xero, QuickBooks, or MYOB subscription, or help you set one up. Software costs are separate from our service fees.

How do you handle Australian and US tax differently?

+

Our team is experienced in both ATO and IRS requirements. Australian clients get BAS/GST and superannuation compliance. US clients get federal/state tax filing and 1099/W-2 processing. The plan structure is the same — the compliance framework differs.

What does onboarding look like?

+

After signing up, we schedule a 60-minute onboarding call, connect to your accounting software, review your chart of accounts, and import any historical data. Most clients are fully onboarded within one week.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

+

Absolutely. You can move between plans at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades take effect the following billing cycle.
