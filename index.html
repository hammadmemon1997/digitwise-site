FAQs

Questions we always get asked.

Everything you need to know before booking a call.

Do you work with Australian businesses only?
Not at all — we serve businesses in both Australia and the United States. Our team is based in Melbourne but works remotely with clients across both markets, handling ATO compliance for AU clients and IRS compliance for US clients.
How quickly can you get started?
After your free discovery call, we typically onboard within 2–3 business days. We'll connect to your existing software, review your chart of accounts, and have clean books ready within the first week.
What accounting software do you support?
We're certified in Xero, QuickBooks Online, and MYOB — and work with Zoho Books as well. If you're on a different platform, just let us know. We'll assess it on the discovery call.
Do you handle BAS lodgement for Australian clients?
Yes — BAS and GST lodgement are core to our Australian service. We manage your BAS preparation, GST reconciliation, and lodgement with the ATO on your behalf every quarter.
How are monthly reports delivered?
Every month you receive a P&L statement, balance sheet, and cash flow summary — all formatted clearly in your preferred software or as a PDF. Growth and Premium clients also receive a brief written commentary on key movements.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely. Your plan can be adjusted at any time with 30 days notice. As your business grows, we'll proactively suggest when it makes sense to move up — and we\'ll never lock you in.
What's your response time if I have an urgent question?
Essentials clients get a 48-hour email response guarantee. Growth and Premium clients are on 24-hour priority support. For genuine urgent matters, we aim to respond same business day.
Is there a contract or can I cancel anytime?
We work on a monthly rolling basis — no lock-in contracts. Just 30 days notice to cancel. We believe in earning your business every month, not trapping you in a long-term agreement.
