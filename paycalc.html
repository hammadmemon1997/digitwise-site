📅 FY 2024–25 Rates

Tax-free threshold $18,200. Rates: 19% to $45k, 32.5% to $120k, 37% to $180k, 45% above $180k.

🏥 Medicare Levy

2% of taxable income. Surcharge of 1–1.5% applies if you earn over $93,000 with no private hospital cover.

🏦 Superannuation

Employer must contribute 11.5% of your ordinary earnings to super in FY2024–25. Rises to 12% in FY2025–26.

🎓 HELP / HECS

Repayments start at $54,435 income. Rate scales from 1% to 10% based on income level.
