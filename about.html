Our Story

We're a Melbourne-based accounting firm serving small and medium businesses across Australia and the United States — remote-first, detail-obsessed, and always on your side.

Why we started
Digitwise.

After nearly a decade working inside large organisations as an auditor and accountant, I kept seeing the same problem: small businesses were getting generic, surface-level accounting that didn't reflect the care and precision their finances deserved.

Numbers were reconciled by totals, not by transactions. Reports were delivered late, or not at all. And compliance — BAS, GST, payroll — was treated as an afterthought rather than a foundation.

Digitwise was built to fix that. We bring the rigour of enterprise-level accounting to small and medium businesses, at a price that makes sense for where you are — not where we'd like you to be.

Based in Melbourne, we serve clients across Australia and the United States. Remote-first by design, available in your timezone, and obsessed with getting every number right.
